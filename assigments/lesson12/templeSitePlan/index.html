Temple Site Plan

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to help those who need a place to stay that is cozy and nice to enjoy there stay. To display information about temples and a place to stay. To display information on temples and the hotel itself.

Target Audience

The target audience is more focused on members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. It will be more focuesed specifically on ages of 18 to old age. People that are planning on staying nights at the hotel. That can afford to pay the price of each night at the hotel.
